# ECONOMICS.md

## What a Converted Lead Is Worth to Credex

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise — sourced from companies that overforecast. The discount is described as "real and substantial." Working assumption: Credex buys credits at 40–60% of retail and sells at 70–85% of retail, capturing a 15–25% gross margin.

A company spending $3,000/mo on AI tools retail ($36k/year) that switches to Credex credits at 75% of retail price:
- Customer saves: $9,000/year
- Customer pays Credex: $27,000/year
- Credex revenue: $27,000/year
- Credex gross margin at 20%: **$5,400/year per customer**

For enterprise customers ($10k+/mo spend): **$18,000+/year per customer**

Conservative average contract value (ACV): **$6,000–$8,000/year**

## Conversion Funnel Math

Working backwards from 100 audit completions:

| Stage | Rate | Count |
|---|---|---|
| Audit completed | — | 100 |
| Email captured (lead gate) | 20% | 20 |
| Opens Credex CTA (>$500 savings cases) | 30% of 100 | 30 shown CTA |
| Consultation booked | 15% of CTA shown | 4–5 |
| Credit purchase closed | 40% of consultations | 2 |
| Revenue per purchase (first order) | $2,000 avg | $4,000 |

**Revenue per 100 audits: ~$4,000**
**Revenue per 1,000 audits: ~$40,000**

At 500 audits/month (achievable with HN + Reddit + organic sharing), that's ~$20,000/month in attributable pipeline, ~$8,000/month in closed revenue assuming standard B2B sales cycle.

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months

$1M ARR = ~$83k MRR = ~42 active customers paying $2,000/month on average.

**What would have to be true:**

Month 1–3: Launch and distribution. HN Show HN drives 1,000 audits. 20 email leads captured. 4 consultations booked. 1–2 customers closed. Revenue: ~$4k MRR.

Month 4–6: Warm channel activated. Credex emails existing customer base (assume 200 companies). 15% complete the audit = 30 audits. 8 high-savings cases. 3 consultations. 1 new customer. Referrals from Month 1 customers begin. Revenue: ~$12k MRR.

Month 7–12: Compound effects. Share link feature drives organic virality — each audit completion has 15% chance of being shared. At 500 audits/month, that's 75 organic shares/month. Tool gets picked up in one "AI tools" newsletter (Lenny's, TLDR Tech). Monthly audits reach 2,000. Revenue: ~$35k MRR.

Month 13–18: Brand established as the "Mint for AI spend." Inbound leads from Google ("AI tool audit" searches). Monthly audits: 5,000+. Revenue: ~$83k MRR = $1M ARR.

**Key assumptions:**
- Average deal size holds at ~$2k/month per customer
- Sales cycle stays under 2 weeks (credits are transactional, not complex enterprise)
- Credex's credit supply scales with demand
- No competitor builds a comparable free audit tool in this window

The single biggest risk is a competitor (OpenAI, Anthropic, or a well-funded startup) building a native "spend optimizer" feature. The 6–12 month window before that happens is the distribution window to capture.
