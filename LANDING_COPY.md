## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free? What's the catch?**
A: Completely free. No credit card, no login. We show you where you're overspending, and if the savings are significant, we'll mention that Credex sells discounted AI credits that could save you even more. You're never obligated to use Credex — the audit is genuinely useful on its own.

**Q: How accurate is the audit?**
A: The audit is based on current official pricing from each vendor's pricing page (sources cited). The recommendations use real usage-fit logic — for example, flagging a Team plan used by fewer than 5 people, or suggesting a cheaper plan that covers the same features. It's not a vague "spend less" suggestion — every recommendation includes a specific reason and savings estimate.

**Q: Does the audit require login or account creation?**
A: No. You fill in your tools and get results instantly. If you want to save your report or get notified of new optimizations, we ask for your email after you've seen the results — never before.

**Q: What tools does the audit cover?**
A: Currently: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Claude, ChatGPT, Anthropic API, OpenAI API, Gemini, and Windsurf. More tools added based on demand — drop your email and we'll notify you when your stack is covered.

**Q: What does Credex do with my data?**
A: Your tool names and savings data may appear in an anonymized shareable link if you choose to share your report. Your company name and email are never shared publicly. We don't sell your data.
